# Product Overview

This is a modern, responsive portfolio website designed to showcase professional experience, skills, and projects. The site serves as a personal brand platform for developers and professionals.

## Key Features

- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with seamless desktop experience
- **Dark/Light Theme**: User-controlled theme switching with system preference detection
- **Project Showcase**: Dynamic project gallery with detailed modal views
- **Contact Integration**: Professional contact form with validation
- **SEO Optimized**: Built-in SEO features for professional visibility
- **Accessibility First**: WCAG compliant with comprehensive ARIA support

## Target Audience

- Potential employers and recruiters
- Professional network connections
- Clients seeking development services
- Collaborators and peers in the industry

## Content Areas

- Hero section with professional introduction
- Skills categorization and visualization
- Work experience timeline
- Education background
- Publications and research contributions
- Project portfolio with detailed descriptions
- Contact information and form
